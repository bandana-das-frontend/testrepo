About us

Argus in its last two years journey has emerged as a channel that champions the cause of poor and downtrodden in the state of Odisha. With tag line ‘In Search of Truth’ and ‘Argus always with the people’, the channel has reached out to the people in the remote part of the state which other established channels have even found difficult to reach. Journalism with courage has been the channel’s identity.

The Argus App

Video news app to get your daily dose of information & news updates from across the world through our secured and trusted sources.

Argus News is a one-stop platform for a variety of hyperlocal content like news from agriculture to politics, daily information on essentials like food and petrol prices, classifieds by local businesses & individual sellers, jobs updates ranging from local to government and life skill tips like finance, education, health, farming and more.

Features:
Local news updates
Get real-time updates on public news and hyperlocal content related to politics, health, sports, agriculture and business in the form of articles and videos in the regional language.
Political updates
Stay up to date with the current elections and latest political happenings in the form of local news updates & alerts across the various cities and districts of Odisha. Learn about all the political headlines and current affairs in the form of articles & videos that you can also share with your friends and family.
Sports news
Get live updates on international and national sports news & more in the form of new articles and viral videos. Find live cricket & kabaddi updates and also latest news on famous sports personalities like Virat Kohli, Rohit Sharma, Mahendra Singh Dhoni, P.V Sindhu, Saina Nehwal and Sania Mirza.
Entertainment news
Get all the latest celebrity gossip and industry news. Read about current and upcoming movies, reviews and get the front row seats while your favorite stars get candid about what they are up to! Find Ollywood news at it’s best.
Online Viral Videos
Latest popular and viral videos at your fingertips to keep you entertained and informed. Watch viral videos and content in Odia from all around the world and share it with your friends and family on WhatsApp, Telegram and other social media handles.
Daily Horoscopes & Astrology
Get free daily predictions and horoscope updates for all the zodiac signs in your regional languages Odia.
Share News Online

Share the news that you read & watch with all your friends and family easily with the easy share button and stream live video news right from the app to various other platforms like Face book, WhatsApp or Instagram. Bring out the reporter in you & become a contributor/journalist and submit stories from around you through your Smartphone & get a chance to feature your articles & videos on our app.

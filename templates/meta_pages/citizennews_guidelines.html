Submission Guidelines
Avoid
  • Hurting religious sentiments.
  • Content that incites violence & creates law and order problem.
  • Vulgar and defamatory material.
  • Reports that violate the dignity of women and children.
    • Media
  • Add more than 1 video or photo to reinforce your story.
  • Shoot in good quality and landscape mode, this mode is better for viewing.
  • Limit the length of your videos to 2 mins or less.
    • Headline
  • Convey the most important and unique thing about your news story.
  • Make it interesting. An article with a catchy headline will get more views.
  • Write your headline using 8 words or less.
  • Use actionable words and an active voice. E.g. ‘India wins world cup 2020’ is better than ‘World cup 2020 won by India’.
    • Description
  • Write in paragraphs.
  • Summarize the article in 2-3 sentences in the first paragraph.
  • Answer all the basic questions, i.e., who, what, where, when, why and how in your write-up.
  • Location and time of incident are important, make sure to get them right.
    • Factual reporting
  • Avoid submitting any fake or false news.
  • Gather all evidence needed to support your story before submitting it to us.
  • Write without bias. Avoid any unfair representation of facts.
  • Do not send news copied from WhatsApp, Facebook or other Social media platforms. Write in paragraphs.
